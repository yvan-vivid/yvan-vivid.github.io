The Present

Attending The Mind of the Artist Conference

This is an attempt at an account of my time at the psychoanalysis conference The Mind of the artist that instead becomes a mere discussion of its premise, and what this premise suggests about the role of the field of Neuropsychoanalysis in interdisciplinarity. While the reader may appreciate this conversation it will leave them wondering about what actually happened during the conference.
